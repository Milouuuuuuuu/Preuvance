## System description

Northstar Support Copilot is a fictional internal assistant used by customer-support agents. It retrieves passages from an approved knowledge base, summarises incoming tickets and drafts a proposed text response. A trained support agent must review and approve every draft before anything is sent to a customer. The assistant does not decide eligibility, pricing, access to essential services or employee performance. The demo team declares a human-validation step and a restricted knowledge base, but has not yet supplied a signed review procedure, a version journal or periodic control-test results.
